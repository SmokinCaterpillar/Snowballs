How does the game work?

You have to throw snowballs at your enemies. Pick an enemy by name or his Ethereum address and throw one of your balls at him. If you score a legitimate hit you are rewarded. A hit is legit in the following cases:

  • A) You and your enemy have more than 0.001 ETH balance.
  • B) Your enemy has an equal or higher level than you.
  • C) Your enemy is not paralyzed from a previous hit.

Throwing balls only costs gas, you do not need to spend 0.001 ETH. This is just a security measure to keep people from opening spam accounts. For a legit hit you will be rewarded as follows:

  • A) You will receive new Snowballs depending on your level:
    • 1 ball if you are level 0
    • 2 balls if you are level 1
    • 4 balls if you are level 2
    • 8 balls if you are level 3
    • ...
    • 3 bonus balls for novel enemies!
  • B) You will receive Snow Gold depending on your level:
    • 1 gold nugget if you are level 2
    • 2 nuggets if you are level 3
    • 4 nuggets if you are level 4
    • ...
  • C) You will increase your level by 1

The bonus balls are only awarded if your enemy was never hit before by anyone! If you get hit, you will suffer the following consequences:

  • A) You will be paralyzed for 30 minutes.
  • B) You will lose 2 levels.

Of course, in case you are paralyzed, you cannot throw balls! That's the game, have fun and happy fraggin!
